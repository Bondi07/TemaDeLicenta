Text about Nikola :

Nikola is a boy who was born in the golden years after the war, of course he didn't know about the war, but the war probably took its toll on him. He was born on October 19 in the city of Kladovo in Serbia, after midnight around one o'clock in the morning. In childhood he was very thin and short, which changed in the future (in terms of weight loss). Since he was a child, Nikola always wanted to become a footballer and coached football for his club in the city, "Djerdap". He had many football idols and because of his love for football, he started training. He coached football for 8 years, but, unfortunately, the time has come for Nikola to choose whether he will go to school and learn something new or choose football as his life's work. Of course, Nikola didn't want to play with his life, so he chose school. He finished primary and secondary school in his hometown, in Kladovo, and enrolled in university abroad, i.e. in Romania. In childhood, Nikola was very diligent, which remained when he grew up. While he was in primary and secondary school, he helped his parents in the shop at the green market in Kladovo where his parents worked and where he works now. Nikola likes good cars, good food, beautiful women, he likes social sports, he likes to travel and discover new things, meet new friends and learn something new. In childhood, he did not travel much due to financial reasons that changed over time, so later he started to travel and meet new friends and new countries.

At the end of high school, he received his diploma, but Nikola wanted to study further, so he enrolled in the Faculty. He wanted to learn programming, so he enrolled in the Faculty of Informatics.
