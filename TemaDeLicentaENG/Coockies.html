"BONDI" SITE COOKIES POLICY

Version updated in May 2023.

The protection of your personal data is important to us, therefore we pay special attention to the protection of the personal data of all those with whom we carry out commercial operations, as well as the privacy of visitors who access the "BONDI" website, in accordance with the Regulation ( EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council, dated April 27, 2016 regarding the processing of personal data and the free movement of such data.

1. What cookies we have and how they are used: Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device by the websites you visit. Your web browser sends these cookies back to the website that set them on each subsequent visit, so that they can make the website work more efficiently, as well as provide useful information such as the way you use the website. The data we collect on the "BONDI" website through the use of cookie modules helps us to better understand our customers, so that we can offer you a more user-oriented experience. Using knowledge of your previous visits to our site, we can improve subsequent visits by adapting our content to meet your requirements. We use cookies mainly because we want to make our websites and mobile applications easy to use and we are interested in the anonymous behavior of users. In general, our cookies do not store sensitive or personally identifiable information, such as your name and address or debit/credit card details. Depending on the duration of their use, Cookies can be classified as "for sessions" or "persistent":
  • Session cookies - remain on the device used for as long as your online session lasts and disappear from the computer or device when you close the browser.
  • Persistent cookies - remain on the computer or device after the browser is closed for a period of time set by the cookie.


The "BONDI" site uses persistent cookies
We use the cookie modules (and the resulting data) for the purposes described in the Privacy Policy.

2. How cookies are used on the BODNI.rs website

The main purposes for which cookies are used are the following:


2.1. Strictly necessary cookies


  • These cookies are essential for the website to function.

  • These cookies do not collect information about you that could be used for marketing or analysis (see below).


2.2. Performance cookies


  • These cookies collect information about how visitors use a website, for example, which of the SDV pages are accessed more often by visitors.
  • They can also show us from which web page or email the visitors arrived at our site and if you opened an email we sent you.

2.3. Functional cookies


  • These cookies allow our website to store the choices you have made about how you want it to work. Examples would be to remember if you have acknowledged the notification regarding the cookie policy or if you have already participated in a study, so you know not to show these messages to you again.

2.4. Analytics cookies


  • These cookies are used to collect information about how visitors access and use our website. It is used to monitor performance, to report and to provide data that will influence improvements to the site.
  • Cookies collect information in an anonymous form, including the number of people who visit the site, their gender and age, the number of pages they have analyzed, which pages they have visited and how long they have spent on the site.

If you do not allow these cookies, you will have less targeted content.

3. What kind of personal data are accessed through cookies?


Personal data accessed through the use of Cookies are collected only for a facilitates certain functionalities for the user. This data is encrypted in a way that makes it impossible for unauthorized persons to access them. A cookie contains information which make the connection between a web-browser (the user) and a specific web-server (the website). If a browser accesses that web-server again, it can read the information already stored and react accordingly. Cookies ensure the visitors of the "BONDI" website a pleasant experience navigation and support the efforts of many websites to offer comfortable services to users: eg - preferences in terms of online privacy, website language options, etc.

4. Control, deactivation and deletion of cookies


Most cookies can be controlled from your browser settings. Can you:
  • Consult the individual cookies together with their expiration date and delete them.
  • Block third-party cookies (or request that they not be tracked).
  • Block all cookies.
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser.

If you choose to block all cookies, many websites will not work properly and functionality may suffer.
For this reason, we do not recommend disabling cookies when using our website.
